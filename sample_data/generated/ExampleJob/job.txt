POSITION SUMMARY
================================================================================
We are seeking a Senior Data Analyst to support a critical public-sector
modernization initiative. The successful candidate will analyze large datasets,
build interactive dashboards, and provide actionable insights to agency
leadership. This role requires strong technical skills, excellent communication,
and experience working in government or regulated environments.

================================================================================
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================
• Analyze structured and unstructured data from multiple agency systems
• Design and build interactive dashboards using Tableau or Power BI
• Write complex SQL queries to extract, transform, and validate data
• Develop Python scripts for data cleaning, automation, and statistical analysis
• Create clear visualizations and reports for non-technical stakeholders
• Collaborate with IT, policy, and program teams to define data requirements
• Document data sources, methodologies, and analytical processes
• Support data governance and quality assurance initiatives
• Present findings to senior leadership and external partners

================================================================================
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
================================================================================
• Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
• 5+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
• Advanced proficiency in SQL (complex joins, window functions, CTEs)
• Strong experience with Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, scikit-learn)
• Expertise in Tableau or Power BI dashboard development
• Experience with large datasets (1M+ records)
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills
• Ability to translate technical findings for non-technical audiences

================================================================================
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
